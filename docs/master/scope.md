# Master Scope

## Product summary
A web-based multi-tenant AI platform for Indian MSMEs that supports structured operational workflows across finance, collections, compliance, and loan readiness.

## Goals
- Reduce manual finance and compliance work.
- Improve invoice collections and cashflow visibility.
- Improve preparedness for lender engagement.
- Create a controlled AI layer that operates with guardrails.
- Serve multiple stakeholders with role-specific interfaces.

## In-scope domains
- Tenant setup and organization profile
- User and role management
- Customer and invoice records
- CSV-based data import for v1
- Collections workflow orchestration
- Cashflow summaries and risk flags
- Compliance workspace with checklisting
- Loan-readiness workspace
- Notification and communication templates
- Explainable AI recommendations
- Admin and auditability

## Out of scope for v1
- Fully autonomous filing or financial execution
- Direct GST submission
- Live ERP/bank integrations beyond approved connectors
- Open-ended low-code builder for external customers
- Native mobile app
- Voice-first experiences
